IRAQ

العراق

Iraq is a Middle Eastern country bordered by six nations, with its capital and largest city being Baghdad. It is home to ancient civilizations, the Tigris and Euphrates rivers, and has the world's fifth-largest oil reserves. Official languages are Arabic and Kurdish, and the majority of the population is Muslim.
